SIS (Soft Is Strong)

Katseye's debut EP, SIS (Soft Is Strong), released on August 16, 2024, introduces the group’s unique sound, blending K-pop and Asian pop styles with themes of emotional resilience and strength through softness. The album title reflects this duality, aiming to show that vulnerability can coexist with power. Recorded across various global studios in locations like Hong Kong, Istanbul, and Los Angeles, the EP highlights Katseye's ambition to reach a worldwide audience. Each track is crafted to balance intensity and warmth, appealing to a broad fan base with its genre-crossing appeal​

ABOUT THE ARTIST

Katseye, a six-member K-pop girl group, debuted in June 2024 as a collaborative project between HYBE and Geffen Records. Known for their cross-cultural blend, Katseye gained early attention through the Netflix series *Pop Star Academy: Katseye*, which documented their rigorous training journey. The group consists of members Sophia (leader), Lara, Daniela, Yoon Chae, Megan, and Manon, each bringing unique backgrounds and skills. Their debut EP *Soft Is Strong (SIS)* explores vulnerability and personal strength through relatable lyrics and diverse musical styles.
